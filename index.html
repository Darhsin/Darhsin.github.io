About

Dr. Dar-Hsin Chen is Professor of Finance at National Taipei University, where he previously served as Vice President for Academics & Research (2023–2025) and Vice President for Administration (2021–2023), among other university leadership roles. He received his Ph.D. in Finance from the University of Mississippi and his M.B.A. from the University of Wisconsin, following an undergraduate degree in Finance from National Taiwan University.

His research covers risk management, derivative pricing, financial markets, corporate finance, asset pricing, and investment strategies. He has taught Business Finance, Financial Management, Corporate Finance, Investment Analysis & Portfolio Management, and International Financial Management at both the undergraduate and graduate levels, and previously held faculty positions at National Chiao Tung University and Tamkang University.

He has published more than 80 articles in journals including the Singapore Economic Review, International Review of Economics and Finance, International Review of Financial Analysis, Physica A, Applied Economics, Asia-Pacific Journal of Financial Studies, Journal of Business Economics and Management, Applied Financial Economics, Emerging Market Finance and Trade, North American Journal of Economics and Finance, and International Journal of Finance, and is co-author of three books: Financial Risk Management, VaR: Theory and Application, and Financial Mathematics (all in Chinese).
